
## FOIA Requests 
In order to collate relevant data, I am submitting a battery of BC CANADA FOIA requests.


### 0 - Qualifiers
Queries that qualify the pursuant data. This will have a bearing on all data outcomes.
#### A - Definitions
Please consider my request for how the province of BC provincial health authority defines the term 'confirmed re-infection case'.
### B - Sanity Check
Please consider my request for the data set: the total number of confirmed, symptomatic COVID-19 cases WHERE each individual had a confirmed, symptomatic COVID-19 infection greater than 5 weeks before.


### 1 - Gross Numbers
Gross numbers will help in verifying irregularities as well as giving a baseline of cases.

#### A - By vaccine status group by serious confounding conditions
Please consider my request for the data set: the total number of confirmed, symptomatic COVID-19 re-infection cases; RESOLVED BY number of vaccine doses; GROUP BY number of serious underlying conditions. 

#### B - By strain
Please consider my request for the data set: the total number of confirmed, symptomatic COVID-19 re-infection cases; RESOLVED BY number of vaccine doses; GROUP BY strain or variant (unknown variant is a valid category).

#### C - By Variant
Please consider my request for the data set: the total number of confirmed, symptomatic COVID-19 re-infection cases; RESOLVED BY number of vaccine doses; GROUP BY variant.

### 6 - Refined by vaccination and time between re-infection
This will inform the role that vaccination plays in staving off re-infection across time.
#### A - Unvaccinated
Please consider my request for the data set: the total number of confirmed COVID-19 re-infection cases, WHERE each case has a confirmed, symptomatic re-infection; HAVING no covid vaccinations before or after re-infection; GROUP BY time between recovery AND re-infection (in days).

### 7 - Refined by proximity of vaccination to re-infection
How long before a re-infection a person is vaccinated will inform how quickly the vaccine becomes effective and potentially for how long it has an effect.

#### A - 1st dose 
Please consider my request for the data set: the total number of confirmed COVID-19 cases, WHERE each case: had not received any vaccination AND had a confirmed symptomatic first infection AND received a vaccination after their first infection was confirmed AND recovered from their first infection AND then had a confirmed, symptomatic re-infection; GROUPED BY time between administration of vaccination AND the date the re-infection was confirmed.
